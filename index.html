About Me

I am a master student in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University and a Graduate Research Assistant at Visaul Design and Engineering Lab (VDEL) working closely with Prof. Levent Burak Kara.

I received my Bachelor's degree in vehicle engineering in 2017 from Tongji University. In 2016 summer, I was a visiting research intern in Combustion Modeling Lab , U.C.Berkeley working with Prof. Jyh-Yuan Chen.
