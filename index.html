About Me

I am a master's student, studying in a Master of Science in Computer Science program at Georgia Tech. I have a wide interest in AI, robotics, computer vision and design.

Earlier, I earned my Master's Degree in Mechanical Engineering from Carnegie Mellon University in 2019. I worked as a graduate research assistant on AI for design in Visaul Design and Engineering Lab (VDEL). I worked closely with Prof. Levent Burak Kara, Prof. Zhenguo Nie and Prof. Amir Barati Farimani.

Prior to my graduate study, I graduated from Shanghai High School and received my Bachelor's degree in Vehicle Engineering in 2017 from Tongji University. In 2016 summer, I was a visiting research intern in Combustion Modeling Lab, U.C.Berkeley working with Prof. Jyh-Yuan Chen.

Other than that, I have also spent time in University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign and University of California at Los Angeles for undergraduate exchanging programs.
